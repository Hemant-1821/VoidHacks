What is a hackathon?
A creative community of programmers from novices to experienced - come together for 36 hours to make amazing projects.
Who can attend?
Void Hacks is accepting college students and high school students who are 18 or older.
When will acceptances come out?
Acceptances go out in mid March for Indore hackers and a bit earlier for out-of-state applicants.
Is there any payment involved?
No, there is no payment involved the event is absolutely free.
Question not here?
Feel free to email us with any questions at voidhacks@svvv.edu.in we will be happy to answer all of your questions.
When will Void Hacks take place?
Void Hacks will take place on Friday, April 3, 2019, and conclude on Sunday, April 5, 2020.
Do you guys offer travel assistance?
Unfortunately, we are not offering travel reimbursements.
When can I apply?
Applications are open now and will close on March 23rd.
Do you have a Code of Conduct?
Yes. Smile, be nice and have fun, but all the other details can be found in our Code of Conduct.
